Put your figure files in this folder.

Name them figure1.png, figure2.png (or .svg)
and update the src= in index.html to match.

Tips:
- Export from R with ggsave("docs/figures/figure1.svg", plot, width=6, height=4)
- SVG is best (stays sharp on any screen)
- PNG is fine too, just export at 2x resolution (e.g. dpi=144)
